    USD/BDT Exchange Rate WEEKLY

    USD/BDT exchange rate — interbank (WAR) vs BC sell
    Taka per US dollar. Higher = weaker taka.
    USD/BDT — Interbank (WAR) USD/BDT — BC Sell
    What these mean. Both are the price of one US dollar in Bangladeshi taka (BDT). Interbank (WAR) is the weighted-average rate at which banks trade dollars, published by the country's Central Bank. BC Sell is the bills-for-collection selling rate a typical second-generation commercial bank charges customers (usually taken from City Bank or Prime Bank from their website).
